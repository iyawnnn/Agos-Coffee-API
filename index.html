About Agos Coffee

Agos Coffee began in 2023 with a simple goal, to create a space where people could enjoy good coffee, relax, and feel at home. Located in the heart of San Fernando, Pampanga, our café was founded by Ar. Miguel Nozawa, whose love for design and community inspired every detail of the shop's cozy, welcoming vibe.

We believe in the perfect balance of flavor, ambiance, and connection. Whether you're dropping by for your morning caffeine fix, meeting friends, or just looking for a quiet corner to study, Agos Coffee is a space that flows with comfort, creativity, and warmth — just like our drinks.
